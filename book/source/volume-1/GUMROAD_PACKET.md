# Gumroad Packet

## Product Title

256 Bits: Volume 1 - The First Byte

## Short Description

A strange illustrated archive of byte-indexed microfiction: signals, ledgers, field notes, impossible forms, myths, and system errors, assembled as a collectible light/dark art-book edition.

## Long Description

`256 Bits` is a curated cabinet of tiny speculative artifacts. Each entry is addressed from `00` to `FF`, carrying its own generation mark, mode, section, QR target, and art direction. The book is designed as an object, not a feed: part literary collection, part fictional archive, part machine-assisted field report.

This review edition includes the current selected entries, the full design system, front matter, visual taxonomy, memory map, generation map, art register, colophon, certificate, and light/dark PDFs. The final commercial edition should ship only after all 256 entries, QR targets, validation checks, artwork, and rights records are approved.

## Included Files

- Light print/screen PDF
- Dark print/screen PDF
- Art direction registry
- Validation notes
- Optional wallpaper/art-card pack when final art is approved
- Optional certificate of assembly for numbered editions

## Positioning

- For readers who like literary oddities, artist books, speculative microfiction, generated artifacts, archives, catalogs, and beautiful fictional systems.
- The binary `256` concept gives the book its shape: finite, addressable, collectible.
- The light and dark editions are not only color variants; they feel like two different artifacts from the same archive.
